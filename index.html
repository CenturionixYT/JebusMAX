Author: Centurionix

Jebus MAX template for Heroes 3 HoTa

Download: Jebus MAX 0.1

This is a simple modification of Jebus Cross Template, which focuses on unlocking various map objects that are currently disabled on JC and making the gameplay more diverse.

Recommended Settings: The same as Jebus Cross

List of changes to standard JC:
Desert:
- Enabled Ancient Lamps and Ivory Towers
- Ivory Towers value increased
- Enabled Eye of the Magi and Hut of the Magi (limited to 1 and 2)
- Eye of the Magi & Hut of the Magi value increased
- Disabled Seer Hut
Tower:
- Enabled Ivory Towers
- Ivory Towers value increased
- Frequency of spawning Ancient Lamps slightly decreased (due to presence of Ivory Towers)
Stronghold:
- Enabled Pirate Cavern
- Pirate Cavern Value increased
Fortress and Cove:
- Enabled Split
- Split Value increased
All starting zones:
- Enabled Eye of the Magi and Hut of the Magi (both limited to 1)
- Hut of the Magi value increased
- Limits on Red Towers removed, frequency of spawning increased
- Disabled Seer Hut
